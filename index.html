"INNOVATION DISTINGUISHES BETWEEN A LEADER AND A FOLLOWER"

Entrepreneurship Development Cell(EDC),founded as Student Entrepreneurship Development Group(SEDG) in collaboration with STEP-JSSATEN, aims at building a better entrepreneurship ecosystem. With a team of 50+ members within the college and diverse pool of student partners across the nation,EDC intends to motivate students to think out-of-the-box, nurture their ideas and realise their dreams!

OUR VISION

"We are a team of super competent professionals from diverse domains working together to bridge gaps between academics and professional world,in order to connect student to the corporate world and help in the development of India's entrepreneurial ecosystem. Thus, Helping them reach their full potential in whichever path they decide to put their foot into."

IDEATHON


In this intensive, workshop-like experience, with the given problem statements,participants work in teams and use design thinking and innovative learning practices to ideate and collaborate on possible solutions.


PITCH IT


As the name suggests, one has to pitch their business idea in front of an intellectual jury; as a solo participant or in a group. Ideas are judged upon marketing tactics,sales strategies,operational procedures and convincing skills.


CHARCHA


A mini conclave where we have discussions on multifarious topics related to the corporate arena with a panel of professionals having a compelling grip on the topic at hand.All in all, it's a charcha worth a charcha
